Sugar cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375oF.
  2. Stir flour, baking soda, and baking powder together in a small bowl.
  3. Beat sugar and butter, then combine egg and vanilla. Gradually add flour mixture.
  4. Roll dough into balls, place them 2 inches apart onto baking sheets.
  5. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until edges are golden brown.
